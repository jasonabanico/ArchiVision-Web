ArchiVision - AI Extended Metadata + AI Assistive Technology for Archive Images



ArchiVision is an AI Extended Metadata and AI Assistive Technology system
that harnesses a variety of AI technologies to generate
extended metadata and assistive descriptions from the image itself.

It draws upon the precision of Azure AI's Computer Vision,
the creativity of Open AI's Large Language Models,
and the eloquence of Azure AI's Speech Services.
